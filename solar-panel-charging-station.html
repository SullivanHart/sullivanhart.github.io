Solar-Powered Charging Station

A device charging station I am developing for ISU's sand volleyball courts.

The Solar-Powered Charging Station is a project that I am contributing to for Engineers for a Sustainable World. Specifically, the Solar Centric team.
While playing volleyball at the sand volleyball courts with another member of the solar centric team, we realized that there was no where to plug in our phones. The courts are not in a very central area, and people are often at the courts pretty late in the evening. This combination means there are a lot of dead phones, and a high need for a charging station.
Once we found this need, we pitched it to the other members of our club. Then, we spent the next few months designing the charging station. I contributed a lot to this process. Specifically, I created the design for the electronics within the charging station.
We want the charging station to remain in place for long after we graduate, so our focus is on longevity and ease-of-repair. Consequently, we are sourcing parts from trusted manufactures and combining them to make our charging station.
Currently, we are pursuing funding from the Green Initiative's Fund. Once we have sufficient funds, we will move on to assembling our first charging station.
